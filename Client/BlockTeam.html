How To Play!

Press "Enter" to start/continue game.

Press "Esc" to pause game.

Press "<" (Left arrow) to move piece left.

Press ">" (right arrow) to move piece right.

Press "v" (down arrow) to move piece down.

Press "^" (up arrow) to rotate piece.

Press Space Bar to move piece to the bottom.
